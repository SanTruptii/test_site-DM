ABOUT BTS...

방탄소년단 (RR: Bangtan Sonyeondan)

BTS, an acronym of Bangtan Sonyeondan or “Beyond the Scene”, is a South Korean boyband that has been capturing the hearts of millions of fans globally since their debut in June 2013. The members of BTS are RM, Jin, SUGA, j-hope, Jimin, V and Jung Kook. Gaining recognition from their authentic, self-produced music and top-notch performances to the way they interact with their fans, the band has established themselves as global superstars breaking countless world records. While imparting a positive influence through activities such as the LOVE MYSELF campaign and the UN ‘Speak Yourself’ speech, BTS has mobilized millions of fans across the world (named ARMY), topped prominent music charts, sold out worldwide tours including stadiums and has been named as one of TIME 100: The Most Influential People of 2019. The band has also been recognized with numerous prestigious awards like the Billboard Music Awards and American Music Awards.

Members...

RM

Birthdate - September 12, 1994

Kim Nam-joon (김남준), better known as RM (short for Real Me, previously for Rap Monster), is a South Korean rapper-songwriter, composer and producer. He is the leader and the main rapper of BTS, also he is notable for his large input in songwriting and production in the discography in the group. As a solo artist, he has released two mixtapes: RM in 2015 and Mono in 2018.

JIN

Birthdate - December 4, 1992

Kim Seok-jin (김석진), known by his stage name Jin (진), is a South Korean singer-songwriter and composer. He is the vocalists, the visual and also the oldest member of BTS. He’s known for his dad jokes and is also called as “Worldwide handsome”.

SUGA

Birthdate - March 9, 1993

Min Yoon-gi (민윤기) known by his stage name Suga (슈가) or Agust D is a South Korean rapper-songwriter, composer and producer. He is the lead rapper of BTS, also he is notable for his large input in songwriting and is the major music producer between the members. As a solo artist, he has released two mixtapes: Agust D in 2016 and D-2 in 2020. In 2018, Suga was promoted to full member of the Korea Music Copyright Association.

J-HOPE

Birthdate - February 18, 1994

Jung Ho-seok (정호석), known by his stage name J-Hope (제이홉), also stylized as j-hope, is a South Korean rapper-songwriter, composer and producer. He is one of the lead rappers and the main dancer of BTS, also he is notable for his large input in songwriting and production in the discography in the group. As a solo artist, he has released first mixtape Hope World in 2018.

JIMIN

Birthdate - October 13, 1995

Park Ji-min (박지민), referred to as Jimin (지민), is a South Korean singer-songwriter, composer and producer. He is the lead vocalist and main dancer of BTS. He is known for his formidable contemporary dancing and an endearing smile.

V

Birthdate - December 30, 1995

Kim Tae-hyung (김태형), known by his stage name V (뷔), is a South Korean singer- songwriter, composer, producer and actor. He is one of the vocalists, lead dancers and visual of BTS. He is known for his deep, husky and soulful voice. He also acted in the Korean historical drama Hwarang: The Beginning.

JUNGKOOK

Birthdate - September 1, 1997

Jeon Jeong-guk (전정국), referred to as Jungkook (정국), also stylized as Jung Kook, is a South Korean singer-songwriter, composer and producer. He is the main vocalist, center, lead dancer, sub-rapper and youngest of BTS. He is also known as the Golden Maknae due to his exceptional talents. He’s also well known for his video productions which are released under the name of G.C.F (Golden Closet Films).

ABOUT ARMY...

ARMY or A.R.M.Y (아미) is BTS's official fandom name. It was officially established on July 9, 2013 after the first recruitment closed. "A.R.M.Y" stands for "Adorable Representative M.C. for Youth" and it carries quite some meaning behind it, given that "Army" is associated with the military, body armor, and how those two things are always together, the fandom name basically means that fans will always be together with BTS. BTS and A.R.M.Y are almost mirrored mages of each other and they have a relationship that is affected by each other. The symbol of A.R.M.Y is expressed as a door on their viewpoint who is waiting & welcoming BTS on the opposite door when BTS opens a new door.
